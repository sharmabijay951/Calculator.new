What is the area?

Area is the measurement of the space contained within the boundaries of a 2D shape. The CalcPro Area tool makes these calculations fast and accurate.

Geometric Shapes and their Formulas

  • 1. Square: \( A = a^2 \)
  • 2. Rectangle: \( A = l \times w \)
  • 3. Circle: \( A = \pi r^2 \)
  • 4. Triangle:
    \( A = \frac{1}{2} \times b \times h \)
    \( A = \sqrt{s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c)} \)
  • 5. Regular Polygon: \( A = \frac{n \times s^2}{4 \times \tan(\pi/n)} \)
